# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-07

## Overview

BitRoute respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## What We Collect

### Information We DO Collect

**Anonymous Analytics:**
- Page views
- Feature usage
- Error reports
- Transaction success/failure rates (aggregated)

**Technical Information:**
- Browser type
- Device type
- IP address (not stored, used for analytics)

### Information We DO NOT Collect

**We never collect:**
- Names or email addresses
- Wallet addresses (stored only in your browser)
- Private keys or seed phrases
- Transaction details beyond success/failure
- Personal information of any kind

## How We Use Data

**Analytics Data:**
- Improve user experience
- Fix bugs and errors
- Understand feature usage
- Monitor system health

**We DO NOT:**
- Sell data to third parties
- Use data for advertising
- Share data (except as required by law)
- Track individual users

## Blockchain Data

**Public Information:**
- All blockchain transactions are public
- Anyone can see swap transactions
- Wallet addresses are pseudonymous
- We do not link addresses to identities

## Cookies

**We use cookies for:**
- Session management
- Analytics (if you consent)
- User preferences

**You can:**
- Disable cookies in your browser
- The Service may not work optimally without cookies

## Third-Party Services

**We use:**
- Stacks blockchain (public network)
- Analytics tools (anonymous data only)
- Hosting providers

**These services have their own privacy policies.**

## Your Rights

**You have the right to:**
- Know what data we collect
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of analytics
- Use the Service anonymously

- No central database (data in your browser)
