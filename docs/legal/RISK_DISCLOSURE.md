# Risk Disclosure Statement

**IMPORTANT: READ CAREFULLY BEFORE USING BITROUTE**

## General Risks

### Cryptocurrency Risk

**Volatility:**
- Cryptocurrency prices are extremely volatile
- You may lose all invested capital
- Past performance is not indicative of future results

**Irreversibility:**
- Blockchain transactions cannot be reversed
- Lost or stolen funds cannot be recovered
- Sending to wrong address is permanent

### Smart Contract Risk

**Bugs and Vulnerabilities:**
- Smart contracts may contain unknown bugs
- Exploits could result in loss of funds
- Audits reduce but do not eliminate risk

**Immutability:**
- Deployed contracts cannot be upgraded
- Bugs cannot be fixed without new deployment
- Users must migrate to new contracts

## Service-Specific Risks

### DEX Aggregation Risks

**Price Slippage:**
- Actual price may differ from quoted price
- Large trades experience more slippage
- Market volatility increases slippage

**Front-Running:**
- MEV bots may front-run your transactions
- You may receive worse prices than expected
- Setting slippage tolerance is critical

**DEX Failures:**
- Underlying DEXs may fail or be exploited
- Liquidity may be insufficient
- One or more DEXs may be unavailable

### Administrative Risks

**Contract Pause:**
- Admin can pause contract in emergencies
- This prevents all swaps temporarily
- No advance notice may be given

**Pool Configuration:**
- Admin controls pool factor settings
- Incorrect settings could cause bad routing
- Multi-sig admin planned but not yet implemented

### Technical Risks

**Network Congestion:**
- Stacks network may be congested
- Transactions may be delayed or expensive
- Failed transactions still cost gas fees

**Wallet Security:**
- Your wallet security is your responsibility
- Compromised wallets lose all funds
- Use hardware wallets for large amounts

**Browser/Device Security:**
- Malware can steal private keys
- Phishing sites can trick you
- Always verify URLs

## Mitigation Strategies

**To Reduce Risk:**

1. **Start Small:** Test with small amounts first
2. **Use Hardware Wallets:** For significant amounts
3. **Set Slippage:** Protect against price manipulation
4. **Verify Transactions:** Always review before signing
5. **Keep Software Updated:** Wallet and browser
6. **Beware Phishing:** Only use official URL
7. **Understand Mechanics:** Know how the Service works
8. **Don't Invest More Than You Can Afford to Lose**
